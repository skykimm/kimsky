GENESIS GV60

GV60

  • GENESIS
  • V : VERSATILE (The versatility of an SUV)
  • Combination of two-digit numbers representing the vehicle class

The dynamic image is highlighted with the name ‘GV’, which means the versatile (VERSATILE) luxury vehicle presented by Genesis and adding ‘60’, the lowest number among the Genesis lineup model names.

USP

Coupe-type, CUV-style exterior based on ‘dynamic elegance’, Interior design with details differentiated from the space emphasizing “the beauty of white space”

Coupe-type, CUV-style dynamics and most active design emphasizes the high-performance image

  • - Two-lines design quad lamp with progressive image and dynamically shape crest grille
  • - High-performance coupe-style smooth and dynamic profile
  • - Two-line rear combination lamp with a sporty feeling

Floating architecture and GV60’s unique details create a luxurious image in a spacious interior space

  • - Floating console with slim cockpit and flat floor provide a spacious and comfortable interior space
  • - The ccIC-enabled cluster and navigation-integrated display provide a future-oriented image

Provides a differentiated EV driving experience with excellent mileage and minimized charging time

  • - Based on the AWD, maximum driving range of 470km
  • - 10 → 80% charge available in 18 minutes with ultra-fast charging
  • - Applying the heat pump and battery heating system, actual driving distance and charging time secured in winter

Provides premium driving sensibility with best-in-class power performance

  • - Front and rear high-power 160kW motor with powerful starting feeling
  • - Sport plus boost mode provides a high-performance driving environment
Providing an innovative UX and maximizing driving sensibility by first applying cutting-edge new technology that enables communication between vehicle and driver

Application of innovative UX-based biometrics and the latest technology

  • - Through the fingerprint authentication system, vehicle access, starting, and driving is possible only with the driver's biometric information
  • - Always keep the vehicle up to date with over-the-air software updates (OTA Software Update) for the main controllers in the vehicle
  • - Genesis Digital Key gives more convenient access without smartphone tagging

Provides a differentiated driving experience with an emotional touch and new driving technology

  • - Active sound design that provides driving pleasure with emotional sound linked to driving performance
